One‑tap golf shot // prototype

Golf Rival condensed into a single gesture: instead of aim → power → accuracy, one continuous finger motion — press → drag → release. Press anchors, drag moves the landing target across the hole, release fires. Visual + feel inspired by Hunting Sniper: scope crosshair, breath hold, dispersion oval.

Hold to scope: if the finger settles for ~220ms, the camera locks and the scope zooms in for precise aim.

Breath mechanic (opt-in): enable Breath mechanic in Tweaks to add a drain timer once scoped — while breath holds, dispersion shrinks 45%; once it expires the crosshair wobbles and dispersion blows up. Off by default.

Cancel: drag onto the left button and release — the shot is scrapped and the whole target turns red as you hover.

Open Tweaks (top right of the device) to play with target style, wind, dispersion scale, zoom factor, scope sensitivity, breath duration.
