Football Card Battler

A 3-minute tactical card duel. Outsmart the manager across three pitch zones — read the bluff, time your super-sub, win the mind game.

  • Each turn you get energy = turn number. Play cards face-down into Defense, Midfield or Attack.
  • Cards reveal simultaneously — so does the AI's. Highest total power wins a zone.
  • Win 2 of 3 zones after turn 6 to win the match. Zone conditions reveal on turns 1–3.
  • Players have abilities tied to football — captains, pressing wingers, the late super-sub.
