Car Blast!

How to Play

  • 🧩 Drag colored blocks onto the grid
  • 🚗 Clear a path from a car to the top row
  • 🎯 Match block color to car color to drive through
  • 💥 Fill a full row or column to clear it
  • 🏆 Roadtrip: send all cars to advance levels
  • ♾️ Classic: survive as long as possible

Boosters & Power-ups

Straight Blast

Drag onto a car — it blasts straight through the entire column, destroying all blocks in its path. Earns +500 pts.

Color Change

Drag onto a car — randomly changes its color. If it can now find a path, it drives immediately.

Hybrid Boost

Drag onto a car — turns it into a Hybrid that can drive through any color block, not just its own.

Color Bomb

Drag onto any block on the grid — removes all blocks of that color from the entire board. Earns 50 pts per block.
